Locations

Visit Us in Kent or Lakewood!

123 Franklin Avenue
Kent, Ohio 44240

Stop by and visit where Taco Tonto’s began. Founded in 1972 by a man named Mike Miller, aka “Tonto” ran a small food cart outside of a local bar in Kent, Ohio. Though the menu was small, the food was prepared fresh each day and cooked individually for each customer. People enjoyed and appreciated the quality, tailor-made food and thus began the long tradition of very loyal customers.
